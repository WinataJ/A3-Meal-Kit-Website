Meet the Chef

My culinary journey began at age 15 in 1987 with an apprenticeship in Melbourne, where I gained invaluable experience at respected establishments, including working under Executive Chef Mark Haynes.

My career later took me to London's Kensington Place and the Michelin Star awarded Prestonfield House with Chef Kenny Coltman, enhancing my cooking expertise. After traveling and cooking across Europe for six years, I returned to start my own business, focusing on delivering fresh, locally-sourced meals directly to homes.

“My vision is to bring the style of cuisine that I have been able to create all these years to you. Made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and delivered right to your home.”
Kylie

Locally Sourced

Kylie's Gourmet Tucker is Geelong owned and operated. All of our meals are made fresh with produce sourced locally from assorted shops and markets in Geelong. By supporting our local business, we in turn support local business.

The meals are prepared just prior to delivery, giving you the freshest possible cuisine to enjoy. Every effort is taken to provide a variety of choice, including gluten free options, to please all tastebuds.

How It Works

  • Choose the meals you want to cook
  • Customise your meal (you can change proteins and serving size)
  • Add extra items like fresh local fruits or breads
  • Convenient Sunday delivery

This service is ideal for busy families, shift workers, and the elderly, and is compatible with NDIS and AGED CARE packages.
